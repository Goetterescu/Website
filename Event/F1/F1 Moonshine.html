Week one:

The first race of the Championship will take place on the F1 Testtrack. The racetrack itself makes driving on that weekend really easy. Caution should be taken after the last corner. This is the place where new Players will drop and maybe even spawn new vehicles. This situation can summon preventable crashes, but the Moderators can't always stop this from happening.

Week two:

The second week will take place on the Grunwaldring during the weekend from 28-29/08. We will be doing an open track day so people can learn how to drive on the new track. This will be on the weekend before the actual Event (21th and 22th). Further informations will be uploaded during the open track day. The allowed vehicles will remain.

Allowed vehicles:

  • MeanMachine-Race
  • Lotus 70 (any)
  • Other Suggestions can be made on our Discord or my Forum account

General information:

  • There will be one qualification round, which will send each Driver to a certain starting position
  • Two races will be done
    • One race with three rounds and a single round race
      • The three round race will be available once for each Driver
      • The single round race will be used as many times as requested by the Mods
  • The average position of the two races will go into the weekly Leaderboard. The same goes for the cup Leaderboard. Click here if that's confusing you.
  • The starting signal will be given by the Mods
  • The times recorded by the server (which can be found on the RoR-Website) will be used for the Leaderboard
  • Each Week will have two racing days: Saturday and Sunday. Both can be used for racing
  • Ideas related to this Event can be discussed on our Discord

Moderators

Moderators should make themselves a note containing a table. This table should be used to write down every lap of each Player.
Moderators are also present to protect the Event against vandalism and such. Players are allowed to be banned instantly if they're not listening to the Moderators. Players have to follow the Event rules and Moderators are there to enforce the rules. We want a nicely organized and calm Event. This requires the Moderators of this server to be active and responsive.
Moderators are asked to send the lap times to Trucker_Gigi, even if they can be "simply looked up on the website". All Staff is asked to respect other Players and their role.
