Rules:

  • §1
    Moderators are allowed to define new rules and even change existing ones.
  • §2
    Only Players with non default names are allowed to use the Event (Player-X is forbidden).
  • §3
    Players have to use the given tracks and stages.
  • §4
    Driving off-road is only allowed if there's no road to drive on.
  • §5.1
    Only legal and light vehicles are allowed.
    • §5.2
      The moderators will check and notify the spawned vehicles. Banned or illegal mods aren't allowed to be used.
    • §5.3
      Big trucks and vehicles with a mass of over 8 tons are forbidden.
    • §5.4
      No more than 3 axles per car.
  • §6
    "We Can't Be the Judged Ones, if There's No One to Judge Us" - Moderators have to watch over the Event and serve justice if necessary. There will be no justice if they fail to do so. This will not stop the Event.
  • §7
    Moderators are allowed to stop or pause any stage at all times.
  • §8
    Cutting off other drivers is allowed.
  • §9
    Causing a wreck, even if it's not on purpose, is a reason to be banned from current and next stage.
  • §10
    Stages will take place every hour. There are three stages which can be voted by the Players on the game server.
  • §11
    Moderators have to take the times of each Player and use them on the Leaderboard.
  • §12
    Moderators are the only ones that are allowed to start a stage. They have to use "Beholder".
  • §13
    Stages will be launched every full hour, not sooner.

Rules may change during the Event.

General:

As you know from the rules, there will be three stages which we are going to use. Players can vote if they want to. Otherwise the active Moderator will decide which stage will be used next. Judges (Moderators) will be active as often as possible. The Judges will enforce the rules, give a countdown for the start (using Beholder) and take the times of the drivers. The times will be inserted into the Leaderboard, which will be posted on the website on Monday.

Stage one:

Stage one is pretty rough, mainly because of the roads and length. There are also a two turning points, which won't be your friends.







Stage one will go as followed:

  • Start at the intersection next to the village.
  • Launch in direction of the tuna picker.
  • Left to the johnston station.
  • At johnston station, stay right.
  • Drive to the observatory.
  • 180° turn and back to the start. There will be an object which will indicate the turning point. You can choose either left or right.
  • Turn left twice and drive past the small public docks.
  • Turn left in direction of the elk hotel.
  • Left turn to the big loading docks.
  • One 360° around a given object.
  • Next is a 180° around the second object.
  • Cross the finish line.

Stage two:

This Stage is short, no need to be scared.





Stage two will go as followed:

  • Start at the tuna picker dock.
  • Turn left in direction of the second airport. Caution: the intersection is messed up!
  • Go past the lost farm.
  • Drive off the end of the road and down on the runway.
  • Cross the finish line.

Stage three:

This is a standard track and good for lazy drivers who are not in the mood to visit the website and read this. It's simple: Use the default rally track of Helens. Beware of potholes and sand. Special rules: Players will start with a gap of about 30 seconds. The times listed on the RoR-Website will be used on the Leaderboard.
