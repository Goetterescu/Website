Thanks to all the Players who joined our Rally. Here are the times for each Stage and the endresult. The bonus Stage won't count in the endresult if you haven't participated in it. The calculation for the endscore is simple. Here is a quick example:
You have the following positions: S1=2;S2=N/A;S3=DNF. In this case there are three drivers who finished S2 and S3. This means that you are on position four. If there are more than one driver who did not participate or finish, they will share the same position. So your scores would be "S1=2;S2=4;S3=4". The calculation
would go as followed: (2+4+4)/3 = 3,3 = 3. Scores will be rounded! If someone participated in the bonus Stage but didn't finish, the bonus Stage will go into the endscore.
