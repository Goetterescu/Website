Tuners Race

This race will allow Players to tune their carbage (yes, that's a thing - inspired by a german event) and race it on a rather poorly made race track.
In other words, a classic shitbox race.

General Information

Only a few vehicles are allowed to be used and they can be almost completly modified. Please read the rules below for more. We will also be using less popular or known circuits for this event. There are virtually no rules about driving style.

Rules

  • §1.1
    Only the mods listed below are allowed to be used.
    • §1.2
      Moderators are in charge for the event, please listen to them.
    • §1.3
      Rules can be changed during the event.
  • §2.1
    Not every part of the vehicles can be modified. Please do not modify any "illegal" part.
    • §2.2
      You can change the torquecurve, engine, engoption, engturbo, brakes, AntiLockBrakes, TractionControl, wheels, wheels2, shocks(1-3), hydros, axles, interaxles and transfercase sections in the truck-file.
    • §2.3
      You are not allowed to change the mesh or N/B.
  • §3
    You have no restrictions related to how much you change the sections from §2.2, but Moderators still have the right to stop you from racing under certain conditions.
  • §4
    Ramming is partially allowed.
