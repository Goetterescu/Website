FAQ:

  • What can I do on this server?


    A special feature is our roleplay based gameplay. You can do roleplay and experience something new. You won't find a better rp server for RoR. Just follow the guidelines.

  • I want to become a Moderator, what should I do?


    Contact Trucker_Gigi either via the Forum linked above or his Discord. You can also find him in the Discord server.

  • I got kicked for no reason. Why is that so?


    Our Moderators are very strict when it comes to rule breaking. A main reason for being kicked is not following the biggest rule "This is a roleplay-server, so please keep it real". Think about it this way: would you do this in real life? Would you drive like a maniac or fly a plane 20 meters above the city? Keep it real, remember that.
