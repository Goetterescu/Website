Main rules:

Some rules may repeat themselves in different guidelines. I will edit this page over time.

  • §1
    All Players on the server have to follow the guidelines.
  • §2
    It's not allowed to roleplay more than one role at a time.
  • §3
    Switching between different roles during a short period of time is forbidden.
  • §4
    Roleplaying emergency services should be treated with respect. Those roles require a bigger amount of knowledge about everything compared to other roles.
  • §5
    Emergency services have the right of way on any road and intersection. This requires them to make themselves noticeable. Either with special lights or horns.
  • §6
    Emergency services have the right to stop other Players from driving.
  • §7
    Public transportations are allowed to park on the roadside, if they're not obstructing the traffic flow.
  • §8
    Truck Drivers have the right to use any public parking lot to load/unload.
  • §9
    Emergency services are allowed to park on the road if required.
  • §10
    Some roles require Players to announce the start of a shift. In this case, Players are allowed to roleplay only after using a special command.
  • §11.1
    The points system can be used to report traffic violations via the form on this website.
    • §11.2
      All traffic violation reports have to be checked by Moderators.
    • §11.3
      Police officers have to keep the proof images or videos until the end of the month. This way they can defend themselves, if the suspect tries to proof that he did nothing wrong.
    • §11.4
      Points will be set to zero at the end of the month.
    • §11.5
      Points will be set to zero when a Player finished his sentence, which is a 24 hour ban.
    • §11.6
      An amount of ten points will get Players banned.
    • §11.7
      All approved reports have to be documented on the "recent violations" page until the end of the month.
    • §11.8.1
      Police officers are allowed to ignore §12 of the BTL, if they are using a       camera trailer or unmarked police car. Players can either use the Ford or the Gavril.
      • §11.8.2
        Both are allowed to be used for fining speeding Players. But Players have to pass the trailer/car of the officer.
      • §11.8.3
        They can also be used for patrolling or other cases than speeding. But note that judges might dismiss your report.
    • §11.9
      Moderators do have to check all reports made by civilians (even by Moderators). Only police reports by Moderators can be ignored. In other words: Police reports by Moderators don't have to be checked, but civilian reports made by Moderators have to be checked by another Moderator.
  • §12
    These rules can be changed by Moderators at all times.

Points:

Players can feel the consequences of being fined by police officers. Let me introduce you to our points system.
Police officers have the right to report traffic members which are committing infractions against the BTL or role guidelines. Every paragraph of the BTL or the respective role guideline can and will get you fined, if necessary. Players who are roleplaying the police are asked to send us an image as proof, the rules which were broken and the names of the officer and player. Officers and civilians can send us the report using the forms here. Moderators will approve reports on the Discord server and post the current amount of points collected by Players. A single infraction can get you more than one point. Example: doing 70 in a 50 zone will result in one point, but excessive speeding might result in two or more points. Points are getting reset at the end of each Month. Getting ten points or more will result in a temporary ban for 24 hours. This will also reset the points to zero.
This way we can control the traffic on our server. It's an rp server, this means that you should act like in real life.

Justice:

Please read the points system article first, before this. Otherwise you might not understand everything in here.
Players can appeal against cases (traffic violation reports). Please note that a few requirements have to be met first.

  • The Player who wants to appeal against a case has to be either the lawyer of the suspect or the suspect himself.
  • The target case has to be still active. This means that only cases which have been submitted during the current month can be appeal against in the respective month.
  • A judge, which would be any Moderator, should be active.
  • Same goes for the reporter or officer who reported the suspect.

Only if the requirements above are met, Players can appeal against a case. But there are rules to be followed. They can be found here and can be addressed as "Crime Handling Ruleset" or CHR. Please mind reading them:

  • §1
    The judge has to be an experienced Player who knows the BTL, VCG and CHR.
  • §2.1
    Court hearings must not be arranged. Lawyers/Players and the judge are not allowed to have a discussion related to the case outside of the court.
    • §2.2
      Court hearings are only allowed to be attempted by the suspect, his lawyer (if present) and the judge. Interviews can be held after the hearing.
    • §2.3
      The judge must be a Player who knows the case and the guidelines.
    • §2.4
      The lawyer must be a Player who knows the case and the guidelines.
  • §3
    The case can only be dismissed by decision of the judge.
  • §4.1
    The case can only be dismissed if it's false or not containing enough proof. The judge has to publish the respective paragraph when he is dismissing a case. Please read the paragraphs below.
    • §4.2
      False information can dismiss a case.
    • §4.3
      Scam can dismiss a case.
    • §4.4
      False or missing proof can dismiss a case.
  • §5
    Every case can be appealed against only once. A failed appeal will be marked in the recent violations page.
  • §6
    An already dismissed case cannot be turned in again. The points will be removed forever.
  • §7
    Moderations will be forced to confront Players who misused this system. This will happen if rules from §1 and §2 are being violated.
  • §8
    Additional rules can be created if necessary. This way we can ensure that justice will be served the right way.
  • §9.1
    Suspects can enter the court hearing by themselves or with one lawyer.
    • §9.2
      The court room can also be entered by the lawyer without the suspect.
    • §9.3
      The reporter or officer can be present during the court hearing and is allowed to speak up.
  • §10
    Violation points can be transferred between Players.

Public transportations:

The public transportations are a good alternative for using your own vehicle. This role uses: Buses, Taxis, Trains, Planes and similar. The commands used by this role are: -actaxi and -acbus. The current Leader of this role is "Dr. Game".


Guideline:

  • §1.1
    All Players are allowed to park on the roadside if required.
    • §1.2
      The traffic flow has to be kept alive.
    • §1.3
      Parking, in this case, is defined as the time invested into loading/unloading or waiting for passengers.
  • §2
    Buses are allowed to use marked bus lanes or stops.
  • §3
    Buses can park on the area of bus stations.
  • §4
    Planes and trains are the only vehicles allowed to use their assigned areas (airports, railways and such) at all times.
  • §5
    This role requires Players to announce their shift. This can be done by using the special commands above.
  • §6.1
    The vehicles used to transport people have to be save for the road.
    • §6.2
      There must be at least one exterior mirror attached to any side of the vehicle.
    • §6.3
      All useable doors must be able to open and close freely.
    • §6.4
      The vehicle should steer straight. This is not the case if the vehicle exits its lane during a 15 meter drive, without any steering input.
  • §7
    No goods are allowed to be moved. Only luggage of clients.
  • §8
    This role can be stopped by the police and fined at any times. So please respect the rules.

Emergency services:

Police, fire departments and emergency medical services are part of this role. In the case of an emergency they're the ones who are legally allowed to floor it. The commands are: -acpolice, -acfire and -acmedic.


Guideline:

  • §1
    The right of way is saved for this role if required.
  • §2
    The special lights and horns have to be used to ensure a safe crossing of intersections in the case of an emergency.
  • §3
    Other Drivers have to give the right of way if necessary. This requires the emergency services to slow down and make themselves visible for others.
  • §4
    Damaging other vehicles is not allowed. The only exception is when other Drivers don't move, in order to make room.
  • §5
    Emergency respond vehicles are allowed to be damaged when used, but only to a point where they're still considered driveable.
  • §6
    Areas like the entrance of emergency rooms have to be kept clear for ambulances.
  • §7
    Faking an emergency can get members of the role and others fined by the police.
  • §8
    Moderators can ban Players from the police role.
  • §9
    Police role players are allowed to build filters and stop other traffic in order to check different things.
  • §10
    Police officers are allowed to fine other Players for not following a guideline.
  • §11
    Police officers are allowed to fine Players for speeding, only if the other Player is passing by the officer or if the officer is following the other Player.

Truckers:

The truckers are the backbone of the city. They are the ones bringing our stuff from A to B. There are no activation commands but you can request a trucker to help you by using -trucker. The current Leader is "Trucker_Gigi".


Guideline:

  • §1
    Truck Drivers are allowed to use public parking lots to load/unload.
  • §2.1
    Combinations and trucks have to meet some basic rules.
    • §2.2
      Semi-trailers have to be able to move freely around the fifth wheel.
    • §2.3
      Loads are not allowed to be longer or taller as the truck or trailer itself.
    • §2.4.1
      Loads are allowed to reach 2 meters (about the length of the Player) out of the end of the trailer/truck.
      • §2.4.2
        Loading ramps are allowed to be used to hold this kind of loads. The length of the ramp is considered as part of the combination and can be used to load longer goods.
    • §2.5
      Cargo has to be secured so it won't leave the area of the combination.
    • §2.6
      Closed trailers or trucks can be used to transport loads which can't be secured.
    • §2.7
      The vehicle should steer straight. This is not the case if the vehicle exits its lane during a 15 meter drive, without any steering input.
    • §2.8
      If there are damaged parts on the combination, they have to be secured. Combinations which are bigger because of a damaged part which is sticking out are illegal.
    • §2.9
      Damaged tires have to be lifted or otherwise disabled. If that's not possible, you have to replace it as quickly as possible.
  • §3
    Trailers have to be able to be moved freely around the hook.
  • §4
    During a filter, this role has to show the current good and tell the officer the current weight of the combination including the load.
  • §5.1
    This role is allowed to make a special learner drive. There has to be an experienced Driver in the truck during this kind of drive.
    • §5.2
      Learners are allowed to break traffic rules and rules of this guideline.
  • §6
    This role is allowed to drive forklifts and other machines on public roads. Hazard lights have to be used.
  • §7
    Truckers can be escorted by the police role during special hauls. The pilot vehicle has to be equipped with special lighting.
